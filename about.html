About Bloom Salon

With its location on the famous Canon Drive, SE Salon of Beverly Hills strives to meet each client’s request and go beyond their expectations. With over 10 years experience in the hair industry, owner and master stylist Shirin’s professional craft of cutting, shaping, and styling hair has placed SE Salon of Beverly Hills at the top of Yelp’s “Best Salons” list. Her specialties include precision-cutting, dimensional color and highlights. She also specializes in damage-repair therapies including Brazilian Blowout, Olaplex, and Keratin treatments. Most importantly, SE Salon of Beverly Hills utilizes all-organic, eco-friendly dyes with no ammonia. Shirin cultivated her advanced skills at high-end salons and has worked with some of the best stylist teams in Los Angeles. She is a graduate of the renowned Vidal Sassoon Academy and holds multiple diplomas in advanced cutting and coloring techniques. Regardless of your hair type—curly, thick, fine or straight—Shirin has the creative talent and exceptional skills to help you look and feel amazing.
